# TangoDisplay User Guide

TangoDisplay is a native macOS menu-bar app for tango DJs. Connect it to Music.app, Swinsian, Embrace, or JRiver Media Center — or use the built-in player to manage your setlist directly. It automatically detects cortinas and shows a clean fullscreen display on an external monitor so dancers know what genre and who is playing.

## Quick start

**With Music.app:**

1. Install the app (see [Getting Started](Getting-Started))
2. Open Music.app and play a named playlist
3. Click the display icon in the menu bar › **Show Settings Window**
4. Go to **Display** and pick your external monitor
5. Click **Move Presentation Window** › **Toggle Fullscreen**

**With the built-in player:**

1. Install the app and open **Settings Window**
2. Go to **Player** and select **Built-in Player**
3. Switch to the **Setlist** tab and drag tracks in from Finder, Music.app, or Swinsian
4. Go to **Display**, pick your monitor, and toggle fullscreen
5. Press play in the Setlist tab

The dancer display is now live.
